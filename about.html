About

The Australian Collection of Duckweed Clones (ACDC) is a collection of Australian Lemnaceae maintained for research, and available for distribution. It is housed at Adelaide University's Waite campus and managed by the Mortimer and Gilliham labs.

Read more about the ACDC, or get in touch.
