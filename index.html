Welcome

The Australian Collection of Duckweed Clones

We are building a collection of Australian Lemnaceae for research, all of which are available for distribution.

For more information, see our database page. The collection is housed at Adelaide University's Waite campus, and managed by the Mortimer and Gilliham labs.

This site is being developed, so content will continue to be added. We are also preparing a manuscript to describe the collection. In the meantime, please browse our current stock or submit a new clone.
